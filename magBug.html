Magazine Major 2 Group Project

Group project Evolve magazine where we were prompted to involve an analysis on biomimicry in product design and its influences. There is a focus on textures and insect-inspired themes. This was a collaborative assignment for second year computer applications where each group member was tasked with creating certain aspects of this brand identity. The butterfly chair was developed by me and further became the brand-identifying logo for our assignment as it inspired our poster design and further pages inside the Evolve magazine.
