Understanding the physics and functionalities in designing a watering. A first year studio project taking prototyping and developing a segmented view to feel the weight and form this product will take upon development.


Brainstorming in groups for understanding different needs and target users to further develop ideas for the watering can, UISCE is designed to target medium to small scale plant sizes having a comfortable and simple form, which can be stored aesthetically as a vase or simply a stand alone piece
