This second-year studio project focuses on the environment of a bus stop and explores ways to improve it based on observations of aspects that people often struggle with at this location. The chosen focus areas for improvement are the garbage disposal area and the additional seating for students

The use cycle is a analysis of the issues that can be seen around the bus stop. This is then Is further compared to how the product design Saandali can help improve and aid students who are waiting for the bus.

The orthographics of this design are similar to that of a regular bench found on carleton universityâ€™s campus With the addition of a location for the garbage bins to be placed and a more comfortable rounded design to accomadate more students for sitting

The development of this design has went through many steps of ideation, the begining stages of the design was to implement small shelters near the bus stop to provide less crowding in the middle of the walkway and the Main bus shelter. This however transformed into analysing the situation with the garbage bins and how students would sit on the floor while waiting for the bus to arrive. As I further analysed these two problems my design focus changed and different concepts where formed as seen in the sketches.
