Lobelia Is a second year Studio project. Lobelia is a rounded centre piece design for holding up to 4 wine bottles for display. With its unique and curved flower like petal design it indices the user to gather orround to view not only its sculptural form but also the bottles of while that help bring Lobelia,s true from to light with its blossoming effect presenting a new and innovative take on what a wine rack can create.

The design process and development of this wine rack went through many iterative sketches before turning to prototyping using toothpicks and paper as well as foam core. The design under went many changes and iterations as the prompted task for the assignment was to use a lateral way of thinking while also taking into consideration of structural principles of support structures that are seen in bridges and other every day items such as chairs. While analyzing as well as experimenting lobelia was then formed.
