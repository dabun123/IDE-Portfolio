This second-year studio project focuses on making a brush with a scraping feature integrated into the products design. Swift is a brush made to aid skaters for cleaning the blades in a safe manner. the scraping function is integrated to first help with getting ride of any snow build up on the blade of the skate, and the brush is made to dry the blades off.

This board demonstrates the use cycle of the function that the brush provides.

The design development for Swift under went many different integrations before coming to a final concept. I began by testing different styles of brushes and what they could be used for as well as different target groups of users. The focus of skating came from a personal experience with the unsafe ways cleaning skates can be. some skaters simply whip the blades off with there hands as there is not a tool or brush small enough or practical enough to bring on the ice or store in a skating bag. With swift I really thought about how to make it a safe and quick experience when on and off the ice for cleaning skates to prevent any injury while still giving satisfactory results.

Prototype Sculpted out of foam to embody the form and function
